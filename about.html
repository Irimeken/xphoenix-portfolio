About Me

I'm Irimekyen Salome Samuel – an AWS Certified Cloud Solutions Architect Associate, a community leader, and a rising voice in the intersection of cloud security and human rights.

My professional transition into tech is fueled by a love for solving complex problems using scalable cloud architectures while advocating for privacy, safety, and inclusion online.

As the founder of Pride of Sheba, my work supporting sexual and gender minorities in Nigeria has given me a unique lens on identity, resilience, and digital safety, values I now bring into cloud security and innovation.

My vision is to design systems that are not only technically sound but socially conscious, blending AI, cloud, and cybersecurity to protect both data and people.
