About Me

I’m Irimekyen Salome Samuel — a certified AWS Cloud Solutions Architect Associate and an emerging cloud security consultant. My journey into tech was born from a deep passion for building secure, scalable, and resilient cloud environments that empower businesses and protect data.

Before transitioning to tech, I led transformative work as a community advocate and the founder of Pride of Sheba, a nonprofit advancing the rights of sexual and gender minorities in Nigeria. That work fueled my commitment to security — especially where people, identity, and privacy intersect.

Now, I channel that fire into architecting solutions that not only solve complex problems but also protect people. I’m passionate about the intersection of cloud computing, cybersecurity, and the future of AI in threat detection and resilience.

Whether I’m designing cloud infrastructures or pushing for digital equity, I bring intentionality, purpose, and creativity into everything I build.
